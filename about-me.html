About me

Hello World! My name is Shirui Zhang (Chinese: 张师瑞). I’m a second-year Master student at Northeastern University. I obtained my Bachelor of Engineering Degree from College of Biological Science and Medical Engineering of Southeast University in 2020.

Previously, I interned at Tencent, Platform and Content Group(PCG) in the spring of 2021. I developed a watermark camera for Tencent QQ.

I was born in Shenyang(沈阳), Liaoning Province(辽宁省), a beautiful city lying in northeastern part of China, just the same as my university's name. I graduated at Liaoning Province Shiyan High School, where I spent my most memorable 3 years of my life.
